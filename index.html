Gannet Bird

Fascinating Facts About Gannets birdfacts@example.com

Gannets are large seabirds known for their spectacular diving abilities and elegant flight.

  • Gannets can plunge-dive from heights of up to 30 meters to catch fish.
  • They have special air sacs in their face and chest to cushion the impact when diving.

Bird Facts

Impressive Divers

Gannets are known for their dramatic dives, plunging into the ocean to catch fish with incredible accuracy.

Graceful Gliders

They are also elegant gliders, often seen soaring over the ocean using air currents to travel long distances.
